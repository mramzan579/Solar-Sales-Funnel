How Net Metering Works

Spin your meter backwards and get credited for excess energy.

1. Generate

Your panels produce electricity during the day.

2. Consume

Your home uses the power it needs first.

3. Export

Excess power is sent back to the grid automatically.

4. Credit

The utility company credits your account, lowering your bill.

Solar Myths vs Facts

Get the truth about going solar.

Myth: Solar doesn't work when it's cloudy.
Fact: Panels still produce 10-25% of their capacity on cloudy days.
Myth: Solar is too expensive.
Fact: With financing, you can go solar for $0 down and pay less than your current bill.
Myth: Maintenance is difficult.
Fact: Systems have no moving parts. Occasional rain usually washes them clean enough.
Myth: It will damage my roof.
Fact: Proper installation actually protects the roof underneath from weather.
