# Project Summary: Premium Solar Company Lead Generation Platform

**Project Name:** Premium Solar Company Lead Generation Platform
**Industry:** Renewable Energy / Solar Installation
**Business Goal:** Generate highly qualified solar consultation requests, capture commercial leads, and automate customer education through interactive tools.

## Problems Solved
- **Customer Confusion:** Simplifies complex solar concepts (system sizes, net metering, battery types) through interactive UI.
- **Sales Friction:** Acts as an automated sales assistant, pre-qualifying leads with the "Solar Readiness Check" before they contact the team.
- **Unqualified Leads:** By providing instant, personalized savings estimates, only high-intent users submit inquiries.

## Target Audience
- Residential Homeowners looking to eliminate their electricity bills.
- Commercial Entities (Factories, Hospitals, Warehouses) aiming for ESG goals and OPEX reduction.

## Core Features
- **Live Solar Savings Dashboard:** Instantly calculates estimated system size, monthly/annual savings, and payback period based on user's bill and location.
- **System Comparison Center:** Visually contrasts On-Grid, Hybrid, and Off-Grid architectures.
- **20-Year ROI Visualization:** Dynamic animated chart showing the break-even point and cumulative profit zone.
- **Financing Simulator:** Compares out-of-pocket cash costs versus monthly $0-down solar loan payments.

## Lead Generation Features
- **Solar Readiness Check:** A multi-step interactive micro-assessment to pre-qualify incoming prospects.
- **Global Conversion System:** Sticky top consultation bar, floating WhatsApp/Call buttons, and a mobile-specific bottom CTA bar.
- **Exit Intent Popup:** Captures abandoning visitors with a free savings report offer.
- **Smart Contact Form:** Incorporates phone verification with automatic country code detection and length validation.

## Technologies Used
- HTML5 (Semantic & Accessible)
- CSS3 (Vanilla, custom Design System, Grid/Flexbox)
- Vanilla JavaScript (No heavy frameworks, DOM manipulation, form handling)
- `intl-tel-input` (Phone validation logic)

## Mobile Responsiveness
Built entirely Mobile-First. Features touch-friendly calculators, accessible accordions, and a dedicated mobile bottom navigation bar ensuring maximum conversion rates on handheld devices.

## Deployment Readiness
100% Frontend. Zero dependencies. Ready for instant deployment on Netlify or GitHub Pages.
